OUR TARGET CUSTOMERS

As a bakery, the DoBak Donuts & Bakery will have a broad target market. Our main target customers are the local resident that live in the area of Damansara since it is located near the resident and populated areas. People of all ages, children through adults, enjoy the delicious treats from our bakery, and therefore will be incorporated in the target market. Our product is not a high-priced item and, as a result, it will be available to nearly all people regardless of varying incomes. Aside from that, our bakery is also located near business area, making our target customers also consist of business professionals that work and reside in the area of Damansara. Our bakery will be the place for them to have a breakfast in the morning or lunch during their break since it offers a wide variety of simple and ready-made snacks for people that is in a hurry to go to work.
